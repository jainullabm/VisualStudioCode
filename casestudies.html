Quantum Computing Case Study

One year ago Volkswagen and D-Wave announced a collaboration to find an everyday problem quantum computing could solve, settling on traffic optimization, according to Ewald.The car manufacturer had location data from about 10,000 taxis in Beijing and focused the project further on traffic between downtown and the airport. After working together for three months to figure out how to map the data to quantum machinery, the team was able to generate solutions in seconds.When attempted on a regular Volkswagen server, it took more than half an hour to get a good solution, according to Ewald.
